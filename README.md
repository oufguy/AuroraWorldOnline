**WELCOME TO THE AURORA PROJECT**
An **Unreal Engine 5** Passion Project 

This project represents a dedicated, multi-year commitment to pushing the boundaries of what is possible within Unreal Engine. Whether this journey concludes in commercial success or serves as a monumental learning experience, the primary goal remains the same: Innovation through Iteration.

In the spirit of transparency and community growth, I will be providing the C++ Source Code for key systems. Whether you are looking for architectural inspiration or deep-diving into low-level engine modifications, these resources are shared to help fellow developers navigate the complexities of high-performance game logic.

**MY PLAN :**
* **Source-First Learning** : By sharing the underlying C++, I aim to create simplefied complex systems like custom network replication and memory-efficient architecture.
> What i meant by this is : Instead of Replicating Skeletal Mesh --> Send players Mesh ID + Animation ID, so its both lightweight and only runs in player's client
* **Long-Term Resilience** : This is a marathon, not a sprint. The project is designed to evolve alongside the engine, prioritizing stability and performance over quick fixes.
*  **Open Contribution to Knowledge** : Success is measured not just by the final product, but by the technical hurdles cleared and the knowledge shared along the way.

**Q&A: DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY**

* Q: Why Unreal Engine?
> **A:** For its industry-leading **Networking Architecture** and the ability to perform rapid, high-fidelity network testing directly in-editor.
* Q: Why Unreal Engine 5 over UE4?
> **A:** To ensure the project is **future-proof** and to leverage the latest advancements in high-scale replication and stability.
* Q: Why focus exclusively on C++?
> **A:** To maintain **low-level control** over performance and memory, which is critical for scaling large-scale multiplayer systems.
* Q: What is the primary technical goal?
> **A:** To build a highly optimized, **server-authoritative** framework that balances complex logic with minimal bandwidth overhead.
* Q: Why share the source code?
> **A:** To contribute to the dev community and provide a transparent look at the **architectural challenges** of solo MMORPG development.
* Q: How does the solo approach affect development?
> **A:** It allows for a **unified vision** and the freedom to over-engineer core systems for maximum long-term stability and personal satisfaction.
* Q: Are you Using AI or Plan to Use it?
> **A:** That's an Important Thing, I DO Use **AI** but in way to Learn and Do my Theory + Testing in-Editor to confirm my Theory
